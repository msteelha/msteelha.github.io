Safe Ride Shuttle Service Web App

Description

My team designed an app for our University's shuttle service. In our group of 3, everyone participated in higher level design discussion but my official role was primarily in front end development. The Safe Ride currently uses pencil and paper to schedule rides. They asked for an app that would get rid of the paper aspect and make it easy to see if there are conflicts in schedules. Only parties of 3 or less can make a reservation and a reservation can be made at any time.

We chose to focus mostly on making a basic app that was easy to use. It has a login system for Safe Ride workers. The admin page we wanted to have a time line of reservations (one for each van in use) so it was easy to see if 2 reservations were conflicting. The client side is just a form to make a reservation. We wanted to maintain that the dispatcher has almost complete control of scheduling rather than try and automate it. This was mostly because of time (worked on this for a few weeks), but also to make sure the app wasn't hard to learn how to use if the person was used to paper.
