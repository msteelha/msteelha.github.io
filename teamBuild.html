Team Building Web App

Description

The team building app was a three-person group project that we spent 5 weeks working on. The concept was to make a web app that would replace the paper slip our professor handed out at the beginning of class to create our groups. The bigger picture would be to expand it to all types of groups. For the project, we made and admin side that puts weights on each of the questions and then use that in our match making algorithm. the admin first creates the questionnaire and gives the clients the questionnaire ID, then the clients can log in and submit their answers. As the clients submit answers, the admin can sort at anytime to see possible groups in their main screen.
